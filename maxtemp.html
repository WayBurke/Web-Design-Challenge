Max Temperature

Regardless of the cities used for this scatter plot, the data will confirm that cities located within between -23.5 to 23.5 degrees Latitude will have higher temperatures. Those cities lie in the Torrid Zone of the earth which is closer to the equator thereby making those locations warmer.

When comparing the northern to the southern hemisphere, cities located between 0 to 23.5 degrees Latitude will have higher temperatures and there will be a steady decline in temperature as the lalitude increases with a few outliers.
